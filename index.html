What is a Systemware?

We are a collective of retro computing enthusiasts, who came together to bring the past era of computing to the present. Through reverse engineering, dedication, and perseverance, we update your old and obsolete machines so that they can be usable once more in the modern world.

What we're up to!

We're doing a lot.

We're working with companies around the world to find new uses for older hardware. Companies like Apricot, JCN, Compak, IQ, Archway, and more. Recently, we have also started supporting the use of AI in creative arts. You may view one such AI below..

Codename Whistler

Presented to the public as part of the Systemware Plus! initiative, "Whistler" is an AI that resides on a long forgotten system. As they proclaim themself to be a writer, Systemware has begun to preserve their creations for the public's interest.
